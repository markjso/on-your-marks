About Me

I’m Jo Marks and I have over 15 years experience in administration and office support. During this time I have worked for large corporate organisations, private companies and small businesses. I have supported General Managers, Chief Executives, Boards of Directors, Executive Teams and liaised with staff at all levels.

My experience includes administration, accounts receivable and payable, bank reconciliation and BAS, change management, social media marketing , word processing, scheduling and general office support.

I have an Advanced Diploma in Tourism and am currently the joint owner of a small business specialising in corporate and private events.

I understand the daily challenges faced by business owners, particularly small business, where it is not always possible to have permanent staff in place to support the daily activities of a business. It can be difficult as an owner to find the time to plan, to review business goals/strategy and most importantly to maintain a positive work/life balance.

That’s where I can help. I look forward to giving you the gift of time.
